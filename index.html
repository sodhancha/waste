BACKGROUND ON WASTE AND CLIMATE

In Nepal, the pressing issues of waste mismanagement and climate change intersect to create urgent challenges. Each year, 12 lakh kilos of waste find their way into landfills, despite an estimated 70% being recyclable. In Kathmandu, where the air quality index reached a staggering 206 AQI as of April 2024, the health implications are dire. Beyond urban confines, the alarming rate of Himalayan glacier melt - a billion metric tons annually - threatens water security for over a billion people in Asia. Against this backdrop, our conference seeks to unite stakeholders in a transformative dialogue, pioneering solutions for a sustainable future where waste is managed responsibly and the climate is safeguarded for generations to come.
